Introduction

That is the Book of which there is no doubt
a guide for those who are cautious;
those who believe in the Unseen,
steadily build Salat
and who give out from what We have provided them.
Also those who believe in that which We have lowered unto you
and what We had lowered before you:
They are convinced in what comes after.
It is they who have been put on track by their Lord;
It is they who are winners.
